## Marketplace Analysts

Subject: Local vs imported MercadoLibre Chile evidence

Hi,

If you analyze marketplace saturation in Chile, imported MercadoLibre listings can distort the competitive picture. We built Apify actors that expose local/import/unknown evidence, local-filter counts, CLP prices, ranking, and canonical URLs.

The production actor is Atomic Actor 1. It coordinates worker shards, dedupes missions, and reports cost/block telemetry.

Docs: https://jchame.github.io/mercadolibre-chile-scrapers/
